# IS-BE

The concept of an _IS-BE_ is an equation of language paired in a fun duality. The _IS_ is a compound of **I**mmortal **S**piritual and the _BE_ is a compound of **B**iological **E**ntity. It was first introduced to me in the book called [The Alien Interview](https://archive.org/stream/1947RoswellAl.INTERVIEWMatildaODonnellMacElroy/1947_Roswell%20Al.%20INTERVIEW%20-%20Matilda%20O%20Donnell%20MacElroy_djvu.txt). I don't know anything about _aliens_, but I do resonate with the _IS-BE_ concept and I find that framing to be particularly useful. 

## Immortal Spiritual 

I feel like 33 years old is when life truly began for me and when I was reconnected with my eternal identity of who I am, I was shocked and terrified and comforted at the same time. I believe that **each of us are unique expressions of the creator _that created each of us_ regardless of _who you think that may be_.** _I'm not going to begin to try to tell you, but the name that I called out to in order to help me restore my identity after it was taken from me, was Yeshua, and for whatever reason, that person, that name, that frequency, saved me from what I lost. 

## Biological Entity 

I'm a man but some times I feel like a beast and when I am acting out various different scripts for entertainment purposes only, that BEAST in the mirror really does have Bell looking at him through his magic mirror. Alas, but what indeed is a MAGIC MIRROR but not something from fantasy? I am an entity yes, biological yes, so yes, I am a biological entity. Bravo. What's your point? Well, you see.. that's the point. I'm not human. I'm a biological entity for sure. Human is a construct and some people behave in a manner that doesn't seem very human to me, so if we are not humans, then we must be **biological entities**. This is the _only logical conclusion_ that I had after experiencing the Ceaușescu orphanages in Romania. 

## I AM AN IS-BE

I've understood the idea of the IS-BE to be a fractal of the I AM itself. You see, there isn't one I AM. There are billions of I AM. Each are unique divine expressions of their likeness. All in pursuit of a better expression of self identity and self exploration. Our mission while here, is to be in service to others while denying the wants of the self. When I write in all caps **I AM AN IS-BE** I literally see an equation: 

    (I AM) (AN=THAT) (IS-BE=I AM) ===> I AM THAT I AM ===> I AM AN IS-BE

Now, maybe I am wrong in how I understand that. But, **that** and **an** being _alike_ or _similar_ or _comparable_ or _interchangeable_ truly makes for an interesting idea that we may all be **immortally spiritual** in additional to being _biological entities_?
