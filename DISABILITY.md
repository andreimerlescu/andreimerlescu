# Disability 

I am a protected person under the Americans with Disability Act. In fact, the day that my photograph _arrived in America_, July 26th, 1990, was the day that **ADA became law.** I am high functioning and capable of taking care of myself without imposing myself or my disability onto anyone I work with. I'm transparent about it, so if you are concerned, feel free to ask me directly any questions you have regarding it. I was institutionalized in Ceaușescu's orphanages at 6 days old, in what I learned in 2025, was a _state ordered abduction_ in Romania, under Decree 770. My time in the orphanage forged my ability to understand extremely complex systems, operate with integrity and loyalty, and deliver world changing results time and time again regardless of which company that I work with.
